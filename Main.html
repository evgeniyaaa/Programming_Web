Fashion

Canary Wharf Spring Fashion Event, 2016

In a city as smartly dressed as London, even the tube can sometimes look like a fashion show.

Canary Wharf welcomed the arrival of the Spring season with the annual Spring Fashion event. It was a great programme of fashion shows, store discounts, prize draws, live entertainment and much more! Leading menswear and womenswear fashion, jewellery and accessories brands offered discounts of up to 30% off, making it the perfect weekend to update your wardrobe with Spring must haves.







The Thames

The City, 2016

This iconic river is a must visit!

A Thames river cruise is without doubt one of the best ways to see London, meandering through the heart of the city and past so many of its most famous attractions. See and experience the sights and splendour of this great city from the relaxed comfort of City Cruises modern, all-weather boats with open upper decks and spacious lower saloons with panoramic windows.

Tower Bridge, 2016



Cruises along the Thames go every 30 minutes from piers at Westminster, London Eye, Bankside, Tower and Greenwich. Circular cruises go from Tower and Bankside. You'll enjoy a wonderful trip.
